Yummy Alfredo Pasta

Description

This is a simple, budget friendly recipe fit for college students or those who just want a quick and cheap meal.

Ingredienst

  • Pasta of choice
  • Parmesian Cheese
  • Heavy Whippinh Cream
  • Black Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta using directions on box
  2. Put butter and garlic powder in to hot pan. Stir
  3. Add Heavy Whipping Cream. Stir
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste
  5. Add Parmesian. Stir until evenly melted
  6. Add Boiled Pasta. Make sure sauce is evenly distributed
  7. Serve
